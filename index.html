Hi, I'm Eunseo Yang.

I completed my M.S. in Human-AI Interaction and am currently working on applying Large Language Models (LLMs) to solve a variety of real-world challenges within industry.

My research interests lie in Voice User Interfaces (VUI), context-aware systems, and user experience (UX). I am particularly interested in designing intelligent systems that adapt to people rather than requiring people to adapt to technology.

I study how people naturally interact with technology in their everyday lives and explore how AI can seamlessly integrate into those interactions. My goal is to create human-centered experiences that make advanced technologies more accessible, intuitive, and meaningful for everyone.
