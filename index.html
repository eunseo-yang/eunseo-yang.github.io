Hi, I'm Eunseo Yang.

I am a master's student specializing in Human-AI Interaction. My research interests include personalized conversational AI and context-aware computing — particularly interactions with conversational agents such as chatbots and Voice User Interfaces (VUI), and how to achieve more refined personalization between users and these agents.

Previously, I researched tailored feedback for presentation anxiety and voice-driven presentation tools, and proactive engagement strategies of AI chatbots for learning. Currently, my work centers on how users interact with voice-based chatbots in everyday life and the appropriate role for AI in these interactions.
